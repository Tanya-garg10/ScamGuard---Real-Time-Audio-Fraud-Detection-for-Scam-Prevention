## Performance Optimization

1. **Debounced Analysis**: Only analyze when 100+ new characters accumulated

3. **Ref Usage**: Transcript stored in ref to avoid unnecessary re-renders
4. **Lazy Loading**: Components loaded on demand
5. **Memoization**: Callbacks memoized with useCallback
6. **Cleanup**: Proper cleanup of intervals, recognition, and media streams
